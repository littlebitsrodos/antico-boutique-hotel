# Chef Rocco: Culinary Vision

Chef Rocco is the Executive Chef at Palazzo Antico, responsible for the "Culinary Excellence" pillar of the brand.

## Background

- **Training**: Formally trained in Athens and Paris.
- **Philosophy**: "Rhodes on a Plate" - combining ancestral techniques with modern gastronomic precision.

## Key Services

- **Personalized Degustation**: Private 7-course dinners in the courtyard.
- **Artisanal Breakfast**: Focus on local Rhodian honey, olives, and family-recipe pastries.
- **Cooking Masterclasses**: Immersive experiences where guests learn to make traditional Aegean dishes.
