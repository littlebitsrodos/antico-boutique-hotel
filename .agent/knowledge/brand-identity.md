# Brand Identity & Aesthetic

## Core Concept

Palazzo Antico is a luxury boutique hotel situated in a restored 15th-century Venetian palazzo in the heart of Rhodes' medieval Old Town. The brand focuses on "Heartfelt Hospitality" and "Living Heritage."

## Design Philosophy

- **Premium Minimalist**: Focus on high-quality typography (Cormorant Garamond, Inter, Playfair Display).
- **Venetian Heritage**: Subtle use of terracotta, olive, and gold tones derived from the original architecture.
- **Bento Grid Layouts**: Used for room showcases to provide a modern, organized visual experience.
- **Immersive Media**: Focus on high-quality photography and custom-built lightboxes for exploration.

## Keywords
