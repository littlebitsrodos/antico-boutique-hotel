A Suite Fit for a Knight

Named after the Knights of St. John who once walked these halls, The Knight's Suite is the crown jewel of Palazzo Antico. This magnificent 45m¬≤ space transports you to another era while surrounding you with every modern comfort.

The suite features original exposed stone walls dating back to the 15th century, a magnificent hand-forged iron four-poster bed draped in the finest Egyptian cotton linens, and an ensuite bathroom worthy of royalty with both a rain shower and deep soaking tub carved from local stone.

Wake to the gentle sounds of the courtyard fountain and enjoy your morning coffee on the private terrace, surrounded by centuries of history. As evening falls, watch the sunset paint the ancient walls in shades of gold and amber.
