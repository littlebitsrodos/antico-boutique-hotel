Discover

The Medieval Old Town

Step outside our doors and into a living museum. The medieval old town of Rhodes is the largest inhabited medieval town in Europe and a UNESCO World Heritage site since 1988.

Wander through the Street of the Knights, explore the Palace of the Grand Master, discover hidden Byzantine churches, and lose yourself in the labyrinth of cobblestone streets lined with Ottoman-era houses.

Our concierge offers private guided tours led by local historians who bring the town's rich tapestry of Greek, Byzantine, Ottoman, and Italian influences to vivid life.

Gastronomy

Mediterranean Dining

Our intimate restaurant occupies the palazzo's original cellar, where ancient stone arches create a romantic atmosphere for exceptional culinary experiences.

Chef Rocco crafts seasonal menus celebrating Rhodian cuisine with a contemporary twist. Ingredients are sourced from local farmers, fishermen, and our own herb garden growing in the courtyard.

Begin with a tasting of local wines on our rooftop terrace, enjoy dinner by candlelight, and finish with traditional Greek coffee in the courtyard under the stars.

Wellness

The Hammam Spa

Discover the ancient ritual of the Turkish bath in our restored hammam, a hidden gem beneath the palazzo. The domed ceiling with its star-shaped light openings creates a mystical atmosphere for complete relaxation.

Our spa menu draws from Ottoman bathing traditions combined with modern wellness treatments. Experience the ritual of kese (exfoliation), foam massage, and aromatic oils that have soothed travelers for centuries.

Private sessions and couples' treatments available. Complete your experience with herbal tea in our relaxation room.

Views

Rooftop Terrace

Our crowning glory is the rooftop terrace, offering panoramic views over the medieval town, the minarets, the ancient fortress walls, and the sparkling Aegean Sea beyond.

Begin your mornings with a Mediterranean breakfast as the old town comes to life below. In the evening, return for sunset cocktails and watch the sky transform from azure to gold to pink over this extraordinary UNESCO landscape.

The terrace is also available for private events, intimate dinners, and romantic proposals under the Greek stars.
