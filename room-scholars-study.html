A Haven for Curious Minds

Rhodes has attracted thinkers, writers, and scholars for millennia. The Scholar's Study honors this tradition with a room designed for the intellectually curious traveler. This unique 32m¬≤ deluxe room features a curated library of books spanning history, philosophy, art, and local lore.

At the heart of the room stands a magnificent vintage writing desk, positioned by an arched window that overlooks the cobblestone medieval streets below. Watch the life of the old town unfold as locals pass by ancient doorways, cats nap in sunbeams, and history continues to breathe in every stone.

A leather reading chair invites long afternoons with a good book, while the comfortable queen bed ensures restful nights after days of exploration. The signature brass banker's lamp and antique globe add to the scholarly atmosphere.
