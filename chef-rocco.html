The Philosophy

Mediterranean Heart, Venetian Soul

Born into a lineage of Southern Italian artisans and trained in the gastronomic temples of Paris and Athens, Chef Rocco brings a world-class perspective to the historic heart of Rhodes.

His presence at Palazzo Antico is more than a role; it is a residency of flavor. By partnering with local foragers and heirloom farmers, he creates dishes that are ephemeral, seasonal, and deeply connected to the Aegean terroir.

"I don't just cook for guests; I compose memories. Each plate is a conversation between 500 years of history and the fresh catch of the morning."
MIHE Michelin Heritage Excellence
