CUCINA

Welcome to my home.

I am Rocco. I grew up right here, in the heart of Rhodes' medieval town. This palazzo was not always a hotel; it was the house where I ran through the corridors with my sisters, Marta and Miriam. The smell of my mother's cooking in this courtyard is what made me a chef.

At Aria di Rodi, my philosophy is a bridge. I carry my Italian heritage in my techniques, but my ingredients are born from this Rhodian soil. I don't want to impress you with complexity. I want to welcome you to our family table.

My kitchen door is always open. Whether you want to talk about the catch of the day or just taste my grandmother's olive oil, come find me.
