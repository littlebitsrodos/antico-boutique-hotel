Creative Arts

**Painting & Photography**: Capture the light of the Aegean and the textures of the Old Town. Our private courtyard and rooftop terrace offer perfect plein air settings, while the library provides quiet for writing workshops.

**Restore & Reconnect**: Sunrise yoga sessions facing the harbor, meditation in our silent courtyard, and healthy Mediterranean catering. A sanctuary of peace amidst the medieval vibrancy.
